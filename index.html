neo-3

Fully open small language models

by aquiffoo, Brazilian software engineer & LLM enthusiast

Overview

The neo-3 family succeeds the neo-2-345M-C1 and C2 models, introducing fully open models with training data, code, and model weights all publicly available. A technical report is coming soon.

Key Achievement: These models are based on the Mixtral architecture and were fully trained on a single consumer GPU, demonstrating the potential for efficient, accessible LLM development.
